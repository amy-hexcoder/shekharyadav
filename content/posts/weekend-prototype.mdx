Constraints are your friend. Here’s a 48-hour flow:

## Friday night — scope

- Write a one-pager: problem, who cares, what changes if it works.
- Decide the smallest demoable slice.

## Saturday — build

- Next.js + Tailwind for speed.
- Supabase or Firebase for data.
- Ship something ugly by lunch; polish later.

## Sunday — validate

- Share with 5 people who match the target persona.
- Ask for behaviors, not opinions.
- If there’s pull, set up a follow-up for next week.
